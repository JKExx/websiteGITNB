About Us

Welcome to Manchester Green Spaces! We're dedicated to promoting outdoor activities and appreciation for nature among young people aged 18-25 in Manchester. Our goal is to inspire you to explore and spend time in your local green spaces, reconnecting with nature and enhancing your well-being.
